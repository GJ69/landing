About us

Vintage Ferrari Servicing. Our wide-ranging array of services will ensure your vehicle is thoroughly and carefully taken care of. From repairs, to modifications in terms of bodywork, paint and so forth, your Ferrari will be treated with the highest attention to details.

What we do

We are an independent, specialized Ferrari garage which keeps and maintaings beautiful vintage cars over the years. Owners may choose to leave their cars with us so to be assured that they get the highest possible level of care throughout the year, or they may simply seek modifications and unique bodywork adjustments. Our expertise and network allows us to uncover rare parts from all over the world. Our location is undisclosed, however owners may have their cars delivered to their address of preference within 48h from the request.
